Hao-Yu (Max) Hsu

I am a second-year M.S. (thesis) student in Computer Science at UIUC, advised by Prof. Shenlong Wang. My research interests lie in 3D vision, including 3D generation, 3D reconstruction and neural rendering. I am honored to receive Siebel Scholars Award, Class of 2025.

Before coming to UIUC, I worked as a research assistant at Vision & Learning Lab at National Taiwan University for two years. I am fortunate to have been advised by Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang on novel view synthesis and point cloud completion, and Prof. Shao-Hua Sun on object assembly with robot learning.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from National Tsing Hua University in 2021 with the highest honor.
